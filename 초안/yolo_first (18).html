A Note on YOLOv6

A Different Branch

YOLOv6 was developed by Meituan, a tech company, not by the original authors or the teams behind v4/v7/v5/v8.

Industry-Focused

It was designed for industrial applications with excellent performance, but the evolution path presented here focuses on the most influential and widely adopted versions.

Non-Linear Evolution

The YOLO family has multiple "forks" or parallel developments. This presentation follows the most direct lineage in terms of community impact.
