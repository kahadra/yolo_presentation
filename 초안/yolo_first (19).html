Q: Is anchor-free always better or faster?

Often, Yes.

It simplifies the pipeline by removing anchor-related hyperparameters and can be faster due to less post-processing.

But Not Always.

Anchor-based models can perform better on datasets with objects of very specific, extreme aspect ratios where anchors provide a strong prior.

It's a Trade-off

The trend is towards anchor-free for its simplicity and strong general performance, but the best choice can be task-dependent.
