Weakness of YOLOv1

Poor Localization

Struggled with precise bounding box placement compared to two-stage methods.

Difficulty with Small Objects

The coarse grid made it hard to detect small objects or groups of objects clustered together.

Rigid Grid Limitations

Each grid cell could only predict one class, limiting detection of multiple object types in one cell.
