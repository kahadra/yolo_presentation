Q: What defines "real-time" in 2025?

The 30 FPS Standard

Traditionally, 30 FPS is the baseline as it matches standard video, allowing for smooth processing without missing frames.

It's Application-Dependent

Autonomous Driving may require 60+ FPS for minimal latency, while CCTV monitoring might be fine with 10 FPS.

The 2025 Hardware Factor

With powerful edge AI accelerators, the expectation is rising. "Real-time" on the edge will mean 60-120+ FPS even for complex models, with power efficiency becoming the new bottleneck.
